# Writing rules

## Forbidden things when writing a secction

- Never break a section into subsections unless I clearly state it
- All paragraphs MUST be linked to the other through connecting words
- No paragraph shall start with a "title". All paragraphs shall be connected in fluid text
- No bullet points. You shall never include bullet points
- No lists. Neever include lists inside a section.
- No words in bold unless it is strictly necessary
- Do NOT use trace ("-") to start and finish one sentence inside another. Prefer commas, or start another sentence with a point (".")
